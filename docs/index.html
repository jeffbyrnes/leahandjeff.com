Questions for the Bride & Groom

  • What should I wear?

    Keep it classy, but black-tie isn’t required. To give you an idea though, Leah & Jeff will be in semi-formal attire (evening gown & tuxedo).

    Again, ladies, grass + stilettos might not be a fun time, so be thoughful with your footwear.

  • Where should I stay?

    We’ve set up a block of rooms at the Hyatt Regency Cambridge. Please visit our custom booking page to make a reservation.

    If you’d prefer to stay closer, here are two hotels we’ve contacted to get a group rate, please just call & refer to the Burrows/Byrnes wedding:

    • Hilton Boston/Dedham
    • Homewood Suites by Hilton Boston/Canton

  • How do we get to the wedding?

    We’re providing buses from the hotel to the venue. Please indicate with your RSVP that you’d like to ride on the bus.

    They will leave the Hyatt Regency at 4:45pm, so don‘t be late!

    The buses will return everyone to the Hyatt after we’ve danced ourselves silly.

  • What about food?

    We’ll be enjoying some delicious food, provided by Mei Mei . We’ll have a variety of items, so there should be something for everyone.

  • Is this a dry wedding?

    Certainly not. We’re having Hive Events and OFFSITE provide us with liquid refreshments, and will have beer, wine, & a carefully curated menu of cocktails.

  • What else is there to do in Boston?

    Lots! Museums, live music, good food, good drinks, sports events, beautiful parks. The City has a great list of things to do , and TripAdvisor’s list isn’t too shabby either.
